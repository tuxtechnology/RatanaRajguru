The Foundation (2000–2010)

Dr. Ratnaprabha Rajguru began her medical career with a passion for holistic health. Over the first decade, she built deep expertise in treating skin, hair, and lifestyle-related health conditions through personalised care.

Dr. Ratna established “The Edit Clinic” in Viman Nagar, Pune — offering weight loss management, advanced skin and hair treatments, and aesthetic procedures. The clinic quickly gained trust through its ICE philosophy.

Ratna Aesthetic Wellness (2023–Now)

Rebranded as Ratna Aesthetic Wellness, the clinic now represents 26+ years of medical excellence — a trusted name for bespoke, results-driven care in skin, hair, weight loss, and holistic wellness.
